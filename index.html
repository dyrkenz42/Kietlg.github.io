Top 5 Must-Visit Destinations in Japan

1. Tokyo Disneyland and DisneySea

Choosing between Tokyo Disneyland and Tokyo DisneySea can be tough. Disneyland is perfect for families with children, with gentle rides and classic Disney charm. DisneySea is ideal for thrill-seekers and adults, offering exciting rides, scenic views, and diverse dining.

2. Fushimi Inari Taisha and Kinkaku-ji

In Kyoto, Fushimi Inari Taisha is known for its thousands of red torii gates leading into the forest of Mount Inari. Kinkaku-ji, the Golden Pavilion, is a stunning temple surrounded by beautiful gardens. Note: Temples may require an entrance fee.

3. Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle, built in 1583 by Toyotomi Hideyoshi, is a historical treasure surrounded by lush gardens. From the top, visitors can enjoy panoramic views of Osaka city.

  • Rating: 9.4/10
  • Location: Chuo, Osaka
  • Estimated ticket: ₱ 233.78

4. Universal Studios Japan

Located in Osaka's Konohana ward, Universal Studios Japan is a thrilling destination featuring Super Nintendo World™ and The Wizarding World of Harry Potter™. Don’t forget to get an Express Pass for shorter wait times!

  • Rating: 9.4/10
  • Location: Konohana, Osaka
  • Estimated ticket: ₱ 388.31 – ₱ 388.40

5. Mount Fuji and Hot Springs

Mount Fuji, Japan’s tallest peak at 3,776m, is an iconic natural wonder. For adventurers, climbing the mountain is a breathtaking challenge. Alternatively, relax in the nearby hot springs and enjoy serene views of Fuji-san.
